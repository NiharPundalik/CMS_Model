# CMS_Model
This repository contains a comprehensive 3-statement financial model and valuation analysis of CMS Infosystems, a leading provider of cash management and ATM managed services in India. The model has been built from scratch with a focus on replicating institutional-grade financial modeling standards used in equity research and investment banking.

The model integrates the Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement, ensuring dynamic linkages across all financial statements. Key components include detailed revenue drivers, working capital schedules, capital expenditure assumptions, and retained earnings roll-forward, enabling a structured and transparent forecasting framework.

A core focus of the model is understanding the business economics of ATM outsourcing, cash logistics, and managed services, which drive revenue growth and operating leverage for CMS Infosystems. The projections are supported by assumptions around ATM network expansion, banking sector outsourcing trends, and operational efficiency improvements.

In addition to financial statements, the model incorporates a Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) valuation, along with supporting calculations such as beta estimation and cost of capital assumptions. This allows for a forward-looking assessment of intrinsic value based on projected free cash flows.

The repository is designed to demonstrate strong proficiency in financial modeling, valuation, and equity research, making it relevant for roles in investment banking, equity research, and asset management. It also reflects practical application of concepts from the CFA curriculum, including financial statement analysis, corporate finance, and valuation techniques.

Overall, this project showcases the ability to build a structured, flexible, and analytically rigorous financial model aligned with real-world investment analysis standards.
